The Fredonia Computer Science Club - Dedicated to the exploration and application of computer science.

Our Club

Since we began, the Fredonia Computer Science Club has been dedicated to the education of all Fredonia Students in the realms of computer science and information technology. We continue to pursue this goal with no less ambition then we had at its conception.

Our History

The Fredonia Computer Science Club was formed in 2000, and we continue to leave a lasting impact on the campus. The activities and events our club puts together ensures that Fredonia's students are learned and prepared form an every increasing technology driven world.

Our Members

All members of the club are examples of the quintessential computer science and computer information systems students. Intelligent, ambitious, driven, and are the real reason for the club's success over the years.
